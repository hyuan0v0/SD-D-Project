Getting Started

Welcome to Virtual Connections!

This guide will help you learn the basics of Virtual Connections.



You'll learn how to use Virtual Connections to:

  • Keep track of your goals for today
  • Plan your day
  • Be part of a study grop

Let's get started!




Keeping track of your goals

Adding new goals

To add a new goal to the calendar, just click +, type in your goal, and press Enter .



Deleteing a goal

To delete a goal to the calendar, right click on the goal that you wish to delete. Then, click Delete .




Viewing calendar events

When your at the calendar, click on a date indicated with a circle. The dates within a circle are days that you have a scheduled event.

Click the x to stop viewing the scheduled events of the date selected.




Create a study group

Try to make the group name understandable. Start with class name then the topic of study.



Find a study group

Select a group that you want to join in the dropdown list.

Click Join Group button to join the selected group



My study groups

View the list of groups that you joined.

Click Delete remove yourself from the group.
